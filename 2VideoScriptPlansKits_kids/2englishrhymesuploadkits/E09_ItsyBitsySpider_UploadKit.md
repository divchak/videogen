### Why This Outperforms:
| Factor | Existing | **This British Version** |
|---|---|---|
| Falls | One | **THREE falls — the tension grows unbearably** |
| Pip design | Generic spider (often scary) | **Pip is the most adorable, determined, loveable spider in animation** |
| Setting | Generic drainpipe | **Cotswolds cottage garden — roses, brick, garden gate — quintessentially English** |
