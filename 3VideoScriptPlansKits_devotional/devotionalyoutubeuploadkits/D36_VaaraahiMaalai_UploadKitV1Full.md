━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Andal (Kothai) — the only female among the 12 Alvars — born in Srivilliputhur in the 9th century. She wore the flower garlands meant for Vishnu (Parisattam) and insisted on marrying Lord Ranganatha Himself. Worshipped as Bhudevi (Earth Goddess), her Thiruppavai and Nachiyar Thirumozhi are part of the Nalayira Divya Prabandham — the Tamil Vaishnava scripture.

Her father Periyalvar (also an Alvar) witnessed her divine love. Srivilliputhur Andal Temple gopuram is the official emblem of Tamil Nadu.
