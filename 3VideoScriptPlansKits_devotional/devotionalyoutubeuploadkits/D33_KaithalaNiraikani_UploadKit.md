🙏 THE MEANING & BLESSING

This Thiruppugazh is a joyful celebration of **Naivedyam** (sacred food offering). 
Every sweet named — modaka, kozhukattai, appam, jackfruit, honey — becomes a prayer. 
"கைத்தல நிறை" (hands full) means when your hands overflow with love, Vinayagar accepts every offering with infinite joy and removes all obstacles (விக்னம்).

Even Brahma bows at His feet. Even Shiva smiles at His wisdom. 
Bring your hands full — He will fill your life with sweetness.

🕉️ ABOUT ARUNAGIRINATHAR & THIRUPPUGAZH

Arunagirinathar (15th Century) was transformed by Lord Murugan's grace at Thiruvannamalai. He composed over 16,000 Thiruppugazh verses in divine ecstasy — each with unique complex rhythm (chandas). "Kaithala Nirai Kani" is one of his most beloved Vinayagar hymns, sung across Tamil Nadu every Vinayaka Chaturthi for centuries.
