## Regional Highlights

### South India
The next 10 days are dominated by the **Chithirai Festival** in Madurai and the **Thrissur Pooram** in Kerala. These events draw millions of tourists and devotees, showcasing the rich cultural heritage, traditional percussion (Melam), and ornate temple architecture.

### Nationwide Observances
**Buddha Purnima** (May 1) and **Narasimha Jayanti** (April 30) are the two major events observed across all states. Temples dedicated to Lord Vishnu will see special prayers and "Abhishekam" (ritual bathing of the deity) during these days.

### Spiritual Significance
The transition into the month of Jyeshtha often marks the peak of summer. Many of these rituals, such as the river entry of Lord Kallazhagar and holy dips during Chitra Purnima, are traditionally linked to water and the cooling of the deities.
