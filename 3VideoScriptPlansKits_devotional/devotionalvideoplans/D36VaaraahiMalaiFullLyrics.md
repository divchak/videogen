## 🏛️ PART 1 — DETAILED CHARACTER DESIGN: SRI VAARAAHI AMMAN (Base for ALL Scenes)

**Goddess Vaarahi Amman — Ultra-Detailed Pixar 3D Character Bible**

Sri Vaarahi Amman is depicted as a majestic, pot-bellied, emerald-green-skinned Divine Mother with a fierce boar face. Her skin glows with an inner divine radiance like polished emerald jade mixed with molten gold. She has a powerful, curvaceous yet muscular build with a prominent belly symbolizing cosmic abundance and fertility. Her boar face features a prominent snout with two long, upward-curling, ivory-white tusks that shimmer like pearls; fierce, compassionate ruby-red eyes that can blaze with protective fire; expressive eyebrows; and a mouth with pearl-like teeth in a slight, knowing smile that conveys both terror to enemies and motherly love to devotees.

She has **eight arms** (ashtabhuja form common in Tamil tradition), each holding sacred weapons and symbols:
- Right hands (top to bottom): flaming trident (trishula), sword (khadga), pestle (musala), and plough (halam).
- Left hands: chakra (discus), kapala (skull cup filled with nectar), noose (pasha), and abhaya mudra (blessing gesture) or lotus flower.

She wears:
- A crown of golden flames and jewels with a central ruby boar emblem.
- Massive gold earrings, nose ring, and multiple necklaces with rudraksha, gemstones, and a garland of fresh flowers mixed with symbolic skulls (stylized, non-gory).
- Ornate silk saree in deep crimson-red and emerald-green with intricate Tamil temple border patterns, gold zari work, and embroidered yantras.
- Heavy gold anklets, armlets, and waist belt with bells that produce divine sound.
- Her body is adorned with glowing Sri Chakra and Vaarahi yantra tattoos that pulse with energy.

**Vahana**: Majestic golden-maned lion or white buffalo (depending on scene). She often stands or rides dynamically.

**Lighting & Effects**: Volumetric god rays, glowing aura in emerald-gold-crimson, floating mantra particles (Tamil script of "Vaaraahi" and "Aim Hreem Kleem"), dynamic hair and fabric simulation, cinematic depth of field, subtle particle sparks from weapons.

**Expressions**: Fierce when destroying enemies (red eyes, flared nostrils); compassionate and motherly when blessing devotees (soft smile, gentle gaze). All animations use hyper-realistic Pixar-level facial rigging for emotional depth.

**Style Reference**: Pixar 3D (like Elemental or Luca) + South Indian temple art + tantric iconography — ultra-detailed textures, realistic fur on boar snout, metallic weapon reflections, fabric physics, 8K resolution.
