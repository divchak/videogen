## 🔱 DEEP THEOLOGICAL & CULTURAL ANALYSIS OF THIRUVAIMOZHI

### What is Thiruvaimozhi?

Thiruvaimozhi (திருவாய்மொழி — "Sacred Words of the Divine Mouth") is a collection of **1102 pasurams** organized in **100 decads** (each 10 pasurams + 1 summary verse) composed by **Nammalvar**, the greatest of all 12 Alvar saints. It is the single most important work in the **Nalayira Divya Prabandham** — in fact, it constitutes half the entire 4000-hymn corpus. The Sri Vaishnava tradition regards Thiruvaimozhi as equivalent in authority to the Sama Veda — it is called the "**Dravidian Veda**" (திராவிட வேதம்) and "**Tamil Vedanta**."

This is one of the most extraordinary spiritual works ever composed on earth. Written in classical Tamil of extraordinary density and beauty, Thiruvaimozhi explores every dimension of Vaishnava theology: the nature of the Supreme Being, the nature of the individual soul, the path to liberation, the forms of Vishnu at the 108 Divya Desams, divine love, divine knowledge, and the soul's ultimate union with Narayana.
