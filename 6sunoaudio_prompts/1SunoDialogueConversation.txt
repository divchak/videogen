Best Method to Generate Pure Dialogue (No Singing, No Music)

In the STYLE box, paste this:

spoken word, narration, dialogue only, clear speech, no music, no singing, no melody, no beat, no rhythm, no instrumental, pure voice, audiobook style(You can customize the voice, e.g.)
professional male narrator, calm and clear spoken dialogue, no music
dramatic female voice acting, emotional monologue, no background music
natural conversation, realistic male and female voices, no music at all

In the LYRICS box, start every paragraph or character line with one of these tags:[spoken word]
[spoken]
[narration]
[dialogue]
[monologue]

Strong Negative Prompt (add at the top of lyrics):
[no music][no melody][no beat][no rhythm][no singing][no rap][no instrumental][silence in background]
Example - Single Narrator / Monologue:
[spoken word]
Hello everyone, welcome to this story from Mumbai.
[spoken word]
Every morning at 6 AM, he would drink his hot chai and rush to the local train station.
[spoken word]
But on that rainy Monday, something completely unexpected happened.
Example - Two-Person Dialogue:
[spoken word] [Male voice]
Hey Priya, did you check the new project files?
[spoken word] [Female voice]
Not yet. What’s the update? Tell me quickly!
[spoken word] [Male voice]
The client rejected everything. We have to finish the redesign by tomorrow morning.
Extra Tips:

Always repeat the [spoken word] tag before every new paragraph.
Specify voice types clearly: [deep male narrator], [warm female storyteller], [young male], etc.
If faint music still appears, add to Style: pure spoken dialogue, voice only, complete silence
Generate multiple times and choose the cleanest version.
