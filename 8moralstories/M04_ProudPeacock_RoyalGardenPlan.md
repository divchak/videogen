# 🦚 M04 — "Percy the Proud Peacock of the Royal Garden"
### Kids Moral Story | British Setting | Pixar 3D Animation | Narration Only
### Moral: **Pride comes before a fall — true beauty is on the inside**
### Duration: ~4 Minutes | Age: 3–8 | Comedy Level: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## 📖 STORY SUMMARY

Percy the peacock lives in the Queen's royal garden and thinks he is the most beautiful creature in all of England. He spends his days walking past other animals and showing off his tail. He laughs at a plain little sparrow named Pip. But during the Royal Garden Show — the most important day of the year — Percy is so busy showing off that he walks backwards into the royal fountain. It is little Pip who helps him out of the mud and cleans his feathers, teaching Percy that kindness is the most beautiful thing of all.

---

## 🎭 CHARACTER PROFILES

| Character | Description | Personality | British Detail |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Percy** | Large magnificent peacock, brilliant blue-green tail, walks with a strut | Vain, dramatic, obsessed with mirrors and puddles (uses them as mirrors) | Wears a tiny silk cravat |
| **Pip** | Small brown sparrow, simple feathers, bright cheerful eyes | Humble, kind, genuinely happy | Lives in a red postbox |
| **Head Gardener** | Tall thin man, green apron, glasses on the tip of his nose | Precise, proper, very easily flustered | Has a magnificent moustache he combs every morning |
| **Queen's Corgi** | Small plump corgi, royal bow around neck | Self-important but secretly shy | Always following just behind Percy trying to look impressive too |

## 🎬 SCENE-BY-SCENE PLAN

### 🎬 SCENE 1 — The Royal Garden [0:00–0:25]
**Setting:** The Queen's magnificent royal garden — perfectly trimmed hedges, flower beds, a large ornate fountain in the centre, Union Jack bunting for the Garden Show.

**Narration (English):**
> *In the grandest garden in all of England — the Queen's Royal Garden at Kettlewick Palace — there lived a peacock so magnificent, so spectacularly feathered, so incredibly beautiful, that he had given himself a title: the Most Splendid Percy. Percy spent every single morning walking around the garden very slowly, making sure every single creature could admire his magnificent tail.*

**Scene Description:**
- Wide shot of the royal garden in full bloom
- Percy struts in slow motion down the main path, tail fanned out
- Garden animals watching — hedgehog, rabbit, garden gnome statues (Percy walks past one and gives it a nod as if it is bowing to him)

---

### 🎬 SCENE 2 — Percy Meets Pip [0:25–0:55]
**Setting:** Garden path near the red postbox.

**Narration (English):**
> *One morning, Percy came across a small, plain brown sparrow called Pip, who was happily singing on top of the old red postbox. Percy stopped. He looked Pip up and down. He fluffed his own feathers magnificently. "My goodness," Percy said, "you are the most ordinary-looking bird I have ever seen." Pip blinked once and smiled. "And you," said Pip cheerfully, "are the most colourful. But colour isn't everything, you know."*

**Scene Description:**
- Pip singing on the postbox — simple, happy song
- Percy looms over Pip, fans tail dramatically
- Percy turns away dismissively and struts off
- **COMEDY:** As Percy struts away, his tail knocks a watering can which tips over and soaks the Head Gardener — Gardener looks up slowly, glasses dripping

---

### 🎬 SCENE 3 — The Royal Garden Show [0:55–1:25]
**Setting:** Garden decorated beautifully for the show. Crowds of visiting animals and garden creatures.

**Narration (English):**
> *The day of the Royal Garden Show arrived. This was the most important day in Percy's year — the day when more eyes than ever would admire his glorious tail. Percy practised his strut fifty-three times in front of the fountain. He admired his reflection so long that the goldfish underneath gave up swimming and just watched him.*

**Scene Description:**
- Percy doing practice struts — counting under his breath
- Fountain reflection — Percy poses, adjusts feathers, poses again
- Goldfish in the fountain are face-up watching, completely bored
- **COMEDY:** The Royal Corgi practising the same strut behind Percy — copying every move — nobody is watching the corgi

---

### 🎬 SCENE 4 — The Show Begins [1:25–1:55]
**Setting:** Central path — all animals lined up, Head Gardener ready to announce the Most Beautiful Garden Resident Award.

**Narration (English):**
> *The Head Gardener announced that the Most Beautiful Garden Resident Award would go to the creature who showed the most grace and elegance on the garden walk. Percy's eyes lit up. This was HIS moment. He fanned his tail to its absolute fullest — every feather perfectly spread. He began his walk. He was so busy looking left and right to make sure everyone was watching — that he did not look where he was going.*

**Scene Description:**
- Percy's tail fans out — genuinely beautiful — crowd (animals) gasps
- Percy walks forward slowly, head high, eyes going left-right-left
- The fountain edge is getting closer and closer
- Pip sees it — waves tiny wings frantically trying to warn Percy

---

### 🎬 SCENE 5 — The SPLASH [1:55–2:15]
**Setting:** The royal fountain.

**Narration (English):**
> *Percy stepped backward — right off the edge of the royal fountain. There was a magnificent, spectacular, glorious SPLASH. Percy disappeared under the water. A moment later, he emerged — completely drenched, tail feathers plastered flat, waterweed on his head, a goldfish sitting on his beak.*

**Scene Description:**
- Percy steps back, arms out, slow-motion tumble into fountain
- HUGE splash — water goes everywhere — soaks Head Gardener again
- Percy emerges: soggy, flat-feathered, waterweed crown, one goldfish sitting on his beak looking confused
- Dead silence from all animals
- **COMEDY:** The goldfish on Percy's beak slowly flaps its fins and Percy cross-eyes looking at it

---

### 🎬 SCENE 6 — Nobody Helps Except Pip [2:15–2:45]
**Setting:** Percy stands in the fountain — nobody moves. Then Pip flies down.

**Narration (English):**
> *Percy looked around for help. The other birds turned away. The rabbits hopped off. Even the Royal Corgi pretended to be very interested in a flower. Everyone was embarrassed for him — and nobody wanted to get their paws muddy. Nobody — except Pip. The little brown sparrow flew down, found a towel from the gardener's shed, and began quietly helping Percy dry his feathers.*

**Scene Description:**
- Percy looks around desperately — animals all look away
- Corgi smells a flower very intently
- Pip flies down with a towel (drags it heroically — it's almost as big as Pip)
- Pip dabs Percy's feathers gently — Percy too shocked to say anything

---

### 🎬 SCENE 7 — Percy Changes [2:45–3:20]
**Setting:** Garden bench — Pip and Percy sitting together.

**Narration (English):**
> *As Pip worked quietly and kindly, Percy sat very still. He looked at his reflection in the puddle below the fountain. His beautiful tail was damp and flat. But Pip — plain, simple, ordinary Pip — was glowing with a warmth that Percy had never noticed before. It occurred to Percy, for the first time, that the most beautiful thing he had seen all day was not his own reflection. It was the kindness in Pip's eyes.*

**Scene Description:**
- Percy stares at his reflection — droopy feathers
- He looks at Pip — who is humming happily while working
- Percy's expression softens completely
- Percy gently moves the towel to help Pip dry a section he can't reach

---

### 🎬 SCENE 8 — The Real Award [3:20–3:50]
**Setting:** Head Gardener steps forward with the award.

**Narration (English):**
> *The Head Gardener cleared his throat. He had seen everything. He walked slowly to the bench — and he gave the Most Beautiful Garden Resident Award not to Percy — but to Pip. "For the most beautiful act in this garden today," he said. "Kindness, without being asked." Percy looked at the award. Then he looked at Pip. And for the first time in his life, Percy smiled — not for an audience — but because he was genuinely, truly happy.*

**Scene Description:**
- Head Gardener gives award to Pip — a tiny golden acorn trophy
- All animals cheer
- Percy starts clapping with his wings — claps loudly and enthusiastically
- **COMEDY:** Percy claps so hard his tail re-fans magnificently and everyone gasps at the beauty — Percy looks surprised, then laughs at himself

---

### 🎬 SCENE 9 — Moral Moment [3:50–4:00]
**Narration (English):**
> *Remember: You can be the most beautiful thing in the room — but if your heart is not kind, nobody will remember you for long. Kindness is the most beautiful thing of all.*

**Scene Description:**
- Card: **"True beauty shines from the inside"**
- Percy and Pip sitting together, Percy's tail fanned — Pip on top of it
