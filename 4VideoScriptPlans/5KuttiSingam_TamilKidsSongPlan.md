## 4.1 Musical Blueprint

| Element | Specification | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Tempo** | 100 BPM | More energetic than Jilebi (92 BPM) — matches adventure, leaping, roaring energy |
| **Key** | G Major | Bright, open, powerful — the most natural key for energy and joy |
| **Scale** | Pentatonic + Bilaval Thaat | Bilaval = Indian G Major equivalent = bright Tamil adventure feel |
| **Duration** | 2 minutes 10 seconds | Perfect toddler attention span + maximum completion rate |
| **Hook Interval** | Every 8–10 seconds | Maximum dopamine loop frequency |
| **Vocal Style** | Child boy voice lead + Appa deep warm voice + children's chorus | Courage story needs a boy's adventurous voice |
| **Signature Sound** | GARRR! (Lion Roar) | THE signature — first participatory sound in Tamil kids songs history |
| **Secondary Sound** | PAI! (Leap/Pounce) | Jump sound = physical energy = kids jump along |

> **Key Differentiation:** Murukku (1 sound), Jilebi (2 sounds), Kutti Singam introduces the FIRST PARTICIPATORY SOUND — kids physically ROAR along. This is not just audio — this is theater!
