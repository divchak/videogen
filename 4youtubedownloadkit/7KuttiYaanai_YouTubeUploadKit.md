## 🏆 PART 1 — TITLE OPTIONS

> 💡 **YouTube Title Rules:**
> - Keep under **60 characters** to avoid truncation on mobile
> - Tamil script first = ranks in local search
> - English transliteration = Tamil diaspora (UK, USA, Canada, Singapore, Malaysia)
> - "Baby Elephant" = global parent search traffic
> - Emoji in title = higher CTR on mobile thumbnails
