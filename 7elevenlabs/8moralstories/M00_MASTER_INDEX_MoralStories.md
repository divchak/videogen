# 📚 MASTER INDEX — Kids Moral Stories (British Setting)
### Pixar-Style 3D Animation | Narration-Only (No Dialogues) | Multi-Language
### Target Audience: Kids 3–8 Years | Duration: 3–5 Minutes Each | 2026

---

## 🎯 CHANNEL STRATEGY

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Style** | Pixar-quality 3D animation, British village/castle/countryside settings |
| **Format** | Narration-driven (no character dialogues — narrator tells the story) |
| **Languages** | English · Tamil · Telugu · Hindi (same scenes, dubbed narration) |
| **Duration** | 3–5 minutes per story |
| **Moral Focus** | One clear lesson per story |
| **Comedy Element** | Every story has at least one funny moment |
| **Character Style** | British: children in school uniforms, red telephone boxes, cobblestone villages, castles |

## 🌍 MULTILINGUAL NARRATION APPROACH

| Language | Narration Style | Target Audience Region |
|---|---|---|
| **English (British accent)** | Warm, storytelling BBC style | UK, Australia, Global English |
| **Tamil** | Gentle Grandparent storytelling tone | Tamil Nadu, Sri Lanka, Tamil diaspora |
| **Telugu** | Warm village narrator tone | Andhra Pradesh, Telangana |
| **Hindi** | Friendly Doordarshan story style | North India, Hindi belt |

> **PRODUCTION NOTE:** All 10 stories use identical scene videos. Only the narration audio track changes per language. This maximises production efficiency — one animation, four language releases.
