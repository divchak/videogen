# 🍬 M01 — "Honest Oliver and the Magic Sweet Shop"
### Kids Moral Story | British Setting | Pixar 3D Animation | Narration Only
### Moral: **Honesty is always the best choice**
### Duration: ~4 Minutes | Age: 3–8 | Comedy Level: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## 📖 STORY SUMMARY

Oliver, a cheerful 7-year-old boy in a little English village, accidentally takes an extra toffee from Mr. Bumble's sweet shop without paying. His dog Biscuit helps himself to a whole bag! Oliver must decide — keep quiet or tell the truth. When he chooses honesty, Mr. Bumble reveals a secret: the sweet shop was actually a test, and honest children get a magical reward!

---

## 🎬 SCENE-BY-SCENE PLAN

### 🎬 SCENE 1 — The Village Sweet Shop [0:00–0:25]
**Setting:** Cobblestone village street, sunny morning. Red postbox, flower baskets on lampposts. Mr. Bumble's Sweet Shop — striped awning, window full of colourful jars.

**Narration (English):**
> *In a cosy little village called Barleymoor, there stood the most wonderful sweet shop in all of England. It was run by a cheerful man named Mr. Bumble, who kept every sweet you could ever imagine — lemon drops, chocolate frogs, strawberry laces, and his most famous treat of all: the Golden Toffee.*

**Scene Description:**
- Camera pans down cobblestone street to the glowing sweet shop
- Window display: massive jars of rainbow sweets, a sign reading "Mr. Bumble's: Sweets For Good Children"
- Oliver skips up the street, satchel bouncing, Biscuit trotting beside him, nose sniffing the air

---

### 🎬 SCENE 2 — Inside the Shop [0:25–0:55]
**Setting:** Interior of sweet shop — warm amber lighting, wooden shelves floor-to-ceiling with sweet jars, old brass scales on the counter.

**Narration (English):**
> *Oliver had saved up two shiny pennies all week — just enough for one Golden Toffee. He walked into the shop very carefully, because the sweets smelled SO good that Biscuit's nose went absolutely wild!*

**Scene Description:**
- Oliver enters, eyes wide, mouth slightly open in wonder
- He picks up ONE Golden Toffee carefully and places it on the brass scales
- Mr. Bumble wraps it in gold paper and turns to help another customer
- **COMEDY MOMENT:** Biscuit spots an open bag of toffees on a low shelf — he looks left, looks right, then SWIPES the entire bag with his mouth and hides behind Oliver's legs

---

### 🎬 SCENE 3 — The Accidental Extra [0:55–1:20]
**Setting:** Shop counter — Oliver pays, then at the door he feels something in his pocket.

**Narration (English):**
> *Oliver paid his two pennies and said thank you. But just as he reached the door — he felt something in his coat pocket. He put his hand in and pulled out... another Golden Toffee! He must have accidentally dropped it in without noticing!*

**Scene Description:**
- Oliver's hand goes into pocket, pulls out a second toffee — eyes go wide
- He looks at the toffee, then at Mr. Bumble (whose back is turned), then at the door
- The toffee GLEAMS in his hand as if tempting him
- Biscuit nudges Oliver's leg — the stolen bag rustling inside his mouth

---

### 🎬 SCENE 4 — The Temptation [1:20–1:50]
**Setting:** Oliver frozen at the doorway — internal conflict shown visually.

**Narration (English):**
> *Oliver's tummy did a little flip. Part of him whispered: "Just go — nobody saw!" But another part of him — the quiet, honest part — said something different. It said: "But YOU saw, Oliver. And that's enough."*

**Scene Description:**
- **Visual Storytelling (no dialogue):** Two thought bubbles appear — one shows Oliver running away happily; the other shows him looking worried and sad later
- Oliver looks at toffee, then tucks it into his palm, takes a deep breath
- He turns around and walks back to the counter
- **COMEDY:** Biscuit panics — he tries to SPIT the bag of toffees back on the shelf before Oliver reaches the counter — he spits it but it hits Mr. Bumble's hat and falls perfectly back on the shelf. Biscuit wipes his paw across his mouth innocently.

---

### 🎬 SCENE 5 — Oliver Tells the Truth [1:50–2:20]
**Setting:** Counter — Oliver stands before Mr. Bumble.

**Narration (English):**
> *Oliver walked back to the counter, his cheeks going very red. He placed the extra toffee on the brass scales and said — with the bravest voice he could find — that he had found it in his pocket by mistake and hadn't paid for it. He wanted to give it back.*

**Scene Description:**
- Oliver places the toffee on the scales — his hands are slightly shaky
- Mr. Bumble turns slowly and looks at Oliver for a long moment
- His big moustache twitches — and then a huge, warm smile spreads across his face
- He kneels down to Oliver's level
- **COMEDY:** Biscuit quickly wags his tail and nudges Mr. Bumble's knee as if to say "I was honest too!" — Mr. Bumble raises an eyebrow at the dog

---

### 🎬 SCENE 6 — The Secret is Revealed [2:20–2:55]
**Setting:** Mr. Bumble walks to the shop door, turns the sign to "CLOSED," then leads Oliver to the back of the shop.

**Narration (English):**
> *Mr. Bumble did something very surprising. He locked the front door and said: "Come with me, young man. You have just passed the test." The TEST? Oliver had no idea there was a test! But Mr. Bumble smiled the biggest smile and opened a little door at the back of the shop — and behind it was the most magical room Oliver had ever seen.*

**Scene Description:**
- The back room glows with golden light — shelves of sweets that sparkle, a painting on the wall showing ALL the honest children who had ever visited
- On a golden plaque by the door: "The Honest Hearts Wall"
- Oliver sees his own name appear magically on the wall as a star is drawn next to it
- Mr. Bumble points to the wall — rows of names, all children who passed the honesty test

---

### 🎬 SCENE 7 — The Reward [2:55–3:25]
**Setting:** The magical back room — Mr. Bumble hands Oliver a special golden tin.

**Narration (English):**
> *Mr. Bumble told Oliver that every single week, he placed one extra toffee near the scales — just to see if a child would return it. Most children never did. But the ones who did — those were the truly special ones. And special children, said Mr. Bumble, deserved the very best sweets in all of Barleymoor. He handed Oliver a golden tin filled with every sweet imaginable — a whole year's worth!*

**Scene Description:**
- Oliver receives the golden tin — his eyes are as wide as saucers
- Biscuit sits very properly, tail wagging nervously
- Mr. Bumble looks at Biscuit, then looks at the shelf where the bag of toffees sits slightly askew... raises one eyebrow...
- **COMEDY:** Biscuit immediately puts his paw on his heart and looks as innocent as possible — Mr. Bumble chuckles and places a single small biscuit in front of Biscuit. Biscuit eats it in one go and wags furiously.

---

### 🎬 SCENE 8 — Going Home Happy [3:25–3:50]
**Setting:** Oliver skips home down the cobblestone street — golden tin under his arm.

**Narration (English):**
> *Oliver skipped all the way home, golden tin tucked under his arm, Biscuit bounding beside him. And that evening, as he shared his sweets with every child on his street, Oliver understood something wonderful — the happiest feeling in the world is not getting away with something. It is being known as someone who always tells the truth.*

**Scene Description:**
- Oliver distributes sweets to neighbourhood children — all cheering
- Final freeze frame: Oliver and Biscuit sitting on a garden wall at sunset, Oliver polishing a brand-new star sticker added to his satchel

---

### 🎬 SCENE 9 — Moral Moment [3:50–4:00]
**Setting:** Simple warm card background — illustrated storybook style.

**Narration (English):**
> *Remember: Honesty is like a golden toffee. It might feel a little hard at first — but once you choose it, it is always, always sweet.*

**Scene Description:**
- Animated text: **"Honesty is the sweetest choice of all"**
- Oliver winks at camera, Biscuit licks the screen
