## Theme & Purpose

A high-energy, catchy youth motivational anthem praising **CM C. Joseph Vijay** for leading the fight against drugs through the iconic “Start Run, Stop Drugs” Awareness Run 2026 and other strong measures.

**Key Elements Covered:**
- CM Vijay personally leading the “Start Run, Stop Drugs” marathon at Marina Beach
- 65 Anti-Narcotics Task Forces across Tamil Nadu
- 7-Point Anti-Drug Action Plan
- Closure of 717 liquor shops and gutkha shops
- Strong use of Goondas Act against synthetic drug networks
- Youth empowerment through sports and healthy lifestyle

**Special Feature:** The song heavily features CM Vijay’s powerful line:  
**“Start Run, Stop Drugs!” “Sports ah edu, Drugs ah Vidu”** — repeated multiple times for maximum catchiness.
