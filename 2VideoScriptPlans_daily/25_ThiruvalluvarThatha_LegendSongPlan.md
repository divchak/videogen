## 4.4 Vocal Direction

```
CHILD VOICE (Arjun — reverent wonder):
- Most restrained vocal in series — reverence requires quieter delivery
- "OOOOH!" echo: exactly like hearing a temple bell — instinctive, reflexive
- Kural counting: each value delivered with genuine understanding, not just recitation

THIRUVALLUVAR VOICE (Deep bass, ancient, warm):
- The deepest, most resonant voice in the series
- Not scary deep — WARM deep — like hearing wisdom from inside yourself
- Each wisdom line has natural gravity — never rushed

CHILDREN'S CHORUS:
- Echo "OOOOH!" in genuine awe — train them to say it as if they just heard something beautiful
- Wisdom counting: clear, respectful, building in confidence
- Final chorus: full children's choir with nadaswaram — pride reaches maximum

SOUND DESIGN:
- Temple bell for OOOOH!: Use a Suchindram or Madurai Meenakshi temple bell tone
  — longer sustain than a regular bell, deeper resonance
- Ancient Tamil kingdom ambience: distant veena, oil lamp crackle, night birds
- Kanyakumari: actual ocean sound — three oceans meeting = unique sonic landscape
- Dream transition: Binaural gentle drone under the entire dream sequence
```
