## 🎯 STRATEGIC INSIGHT FOR YOUR CHANNEL

### What the Top Channels Have in Common:
```
✅ 1. Simple Tamil words repeated as song title = easy searchability
✅ 2. Cultural food / nature / folk references = instant emotional connection
✅ 3. Bright 3D/2D animation = visual stimulation for kids
✅ 4. Songs under 3 minutes = high completion rate (YouTube algorithm loves this)
✅ 5. Bilingual titles (Tamil + English) = diaspora + local reach combined
✅ 6. Paatti / grandma / family angle = adults share the video
✅ 7. Counting / alphabet educational element = parents promote it
✅ 8. ASMR / sensory sounds (crunch, splash, crinkle) = toddler hypnosis
```
