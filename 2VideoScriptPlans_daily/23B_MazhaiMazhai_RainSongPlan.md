| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Tempo** | 98 BPM — flowing, liquid rain-feel; slightly more meditative than the Maari Maari version |

| **Signature Sound** | TIPPY-TIPPY-TAP! + "Mazhai!" called with classical folk depth and pride |

| **What makes it different** | Veena motif in intro/outro, deeper Carnatic gamakam bends, ghatam anchors the groove |
