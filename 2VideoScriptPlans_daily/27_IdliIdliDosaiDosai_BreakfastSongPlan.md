## 4.1 Musical Blueprint

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Tempo** | 108 BPM — upbeat morning kitchen energy |
| **Key** | F Major — warm, golden, appetizing |
| **Signature Sounds** | சீ சீ! (SIZZLE) + பட்! (PUFF) — both as percussion AND vocal |
| **Duration** | 2:10 |

## 4.5 Vocal Direction

```
CHILD LEAD (Ponni — sleepy → excited):
- Opening: literally half-asleep, voice slightly slurred with sleep → suddenly AWAKE on "Dosai vaasam"
- "Idli Idli!" — first time tentative → second time full joy
- "சீ சீ சீ!" — the sizzle sound: long, drawn out, ASMR quality — linger on it
- "Tummy full!" — the most satisfied sound humanly possible

PAATTI (Kitchen wisdom, quiet pride):
- Bridge section: narrating the farm-to-plate story — reverent for the farmer's effort
- Her kitchen sounds ARE her voice — the sizzle speaks for her

SAMBAR CHARACTER (Excited, boisterous):
- His voice IS the excited bubble sound — effervescent, rushing
CHUTNEY CHARACTER (Cool, measured):
- Calm contrast to Sambar — each line measured and self-satisfied

SOUND DESIGN (MOST IMPORTANT IN SERIES):
- The dosa SIZZLE is the SOUL of this song
- Record actual cast iron tava + fermented batter: the sound is unmistakable
- Layer three sizzle types: tiny (first drop test), building (full spiral pour), sustained (cooking)
- The idli steam release: soft pressure-release sound — deeply satisfying
- Kitchen ambience: wood fire crackle, copper vessel clinks, rooster outside, morning birds
- Aroma visualization audio: a soft warm chord played every time the camera shows steam/aroma
```
