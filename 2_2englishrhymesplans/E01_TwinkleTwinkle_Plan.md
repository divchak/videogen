# 🎛️ PART 5 — PRACTICAL MUSIC PRODUCTION GUIDE

## ⚡ What Suno AI CAN vs CANNOT Do

| ✅ CAN DO | ❌ CANNOT DO |
|---|---|
| Classic melody arrangement | Real sparkle/chime SFX |
| Warm orchestral texture | Authentic "star twinkle" shimmer sound |
| Child vocal approximation | Emotional bedtime warmth of real recording |
| Glockenspiel lead | Perfect diction on child voice |
| Gentle crescendo | Spatial audio for "sky" feeling |

## 🎯 Hybrid Production Approach

```
STEP 1: Suno AI          → Core orchestral track + melody
STEP 2: Freesound.org    → Star shimmer chimes + night ambience
STEP 3: Record           → Real child voice if possible (daughter/niece)
STEP 4: Audacity         → Final mix — pan shimmer L/R for space effect
```
