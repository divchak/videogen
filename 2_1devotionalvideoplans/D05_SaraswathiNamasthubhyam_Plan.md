| Factor | Reason |
|---|---|
| **Saraswati Pooja = school event** | Every Tamil school celebrates Saraswati Pooja — mandatory worship of the knowledge goddess |
| **Exam season searches** | Students search Saraswati songs before every major exam (board exams, entrance tests) |
| **Music learners** | Every music student in Tamil Nadu begins lessons by praying to Saraswati |
| **Navarathri peak** | 9-day Navarathri festival = dedicated day for Saraswati worship = annual traffic peak |
| **Tamil schools diaspora** | Tamil schools in UK, USA, Singapore, Malaysia all do Saraswati Pooja |
| **Students + teachers** | Appeals to the single largest demographic in Tamil society |
