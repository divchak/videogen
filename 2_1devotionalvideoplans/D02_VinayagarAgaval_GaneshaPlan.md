             Veena instrumental bridge → Verse 3 (wisdom teaching) →

                   the word "வினாயகா" always followed by a single clear 
                   veena note like an acknowledgment
[Duration]: 4 minutes
[Lyrical Style]: Avvaiyar's classical Agaval — ancient Tamil verse form with 
                 7.5 beat rhythm (aasiriya virutha agaval)
[Energy Arc]: Opens in quiet reverence → builds in warm devotion → 
              climaxes in the vision of divine wisdom → closes in peaceful blessing
