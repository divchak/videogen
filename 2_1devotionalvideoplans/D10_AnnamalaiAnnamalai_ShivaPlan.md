## 🧠 WHY THIS SONG?

| Factor | Reason |
|---|---|
| **Tiruvannamalai = one of 5 Panchabhuta Stalas** | The sacred Agni (fire) Shivalingam — one of five elemental temples |
| **Karthigai Deepam = massive annual peak** | The Karthigai Deepam fire on the mountain summit is seen for 30km — huge annual festival traffic |
| **Girivalam — monthly full moon** | Hundreds of thousands circumambulate the sacred mountain every full moon — perpetual traffic |
| **Tamil Shaivite identity** | Tiruvannamalai is the deepest sacred identity of Tamil Shaivism |
| **Ramana Maharshi connection** | Ramana Maharshi made Tiruvannamalai globally famous — international spiritual audience |
| **Visually spectacular** | A sacred red mountain + eternal fire = the most dramatic devotional visual possible |
