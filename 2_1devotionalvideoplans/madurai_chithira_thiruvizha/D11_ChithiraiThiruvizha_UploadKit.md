# Madurai Chithirai Thiruvizha: Event Schedule

The festival is divided into two distinct halves: the Shaivite celebrations at the Meenakshi Amman Temple and the Vaishnavite journey of Lord Kallazhagar.

## Phase 1: Meenakshi Amman Temple Events
These events focus on the coronation and celestial wedding of Goddess Meenakshi.

| Event Name | Significance |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Kodiyetram** | The official start marked by the hoisting of the holy flag at the temple. |
| **Pattabhishekam** | The Coronation ceremony where Meenakshi is crowned the Queen of Madurai. |
| **Dikvijayam** | The Goddess travels in the eight directions to conquer the world. |
| **Meenakshi Thirukalyanam** | **The Celestial Wedding** of Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. |
| **Therottam** | The massive Chariot (Car) Festival through the four Masi streets. |

---

## Phase 2: Lord Kallazhagar Events
These events follow Lord Vishnu (as Kallazhagar) as he travels from the Alagar Hills into the heart of Madurai.

| Event Name | Significance |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Ethir Sevai** | The "receiving" ceremony where the people of Madurai welcome the Lord. |
| **Vaigai Eluntharulal** | **The Peak Event:** Lord Kallazhagar enters the Vaigai River on a golden horse. |
| **Vandiyur Festival** | The Lord visits the Vandiyur Mariamman Teppakulam for specific rituals. |
| **Dasavatharam** | The Lord is displayed in his ten incarnations throughout the night. |
| **Poo Pallakku** | The "Flower Palanquin" procession as the Lord begins his journey back. |
| **Return to Alagar Koil** | The Lord arrives back at his hillside abode, concluding the festival. |
