## 💡 PART 9 — LAUNCH STRATEGY

### Three Launch Hooks:

**Hook 1: THE CHOICE MOMENT (Curiosity)**
> Post the Scene 4 doorway moment (Oliver frozen, looking at the toffee then at the door) as a 15-second Short/Reel: *"He could have kept it. Nobody was watching. What would you do? 🍬"* — Full story [link]

**Hook 2: THE SURPRISE REVEAL (Storytelling)**
