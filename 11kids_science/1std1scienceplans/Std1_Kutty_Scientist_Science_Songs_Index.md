# 🌱 Kutty Scientist — Standard 1 Science Songs
## Complete Series Index & Production Hub (10 Songs)

**Series Tagline:** *“Every child is a scientist! — ஒவ்வொரு குழந்தையும் ஒரு விஞ்ஞானி!”*

### 📚 Series Overview
**Kutty Scientist** is a joyful, high-quality bilingual (Tamil + English) educational YouTube series designed specifically for **Standard 1 / Class 1** children (ages 4–7) following the **Tamil Nadu State Board Science curriculum**.

Each 3-minute-30-second song transforms core science concepts into:
- Catchy, sing-along bilingual lyrics
- Vibrant **3D Pixar-style animation** with lovable recurring characters
- Interactive quizzes, gestures, and “You are a SCIENTIST!” empowerment moments
- Clear **English-only on-screen text** (for universal clarity + early English exposure)
- Strong Tamil cultural warmth and pronunciation accuracy

**Target Outcomes:**
- Children can classify, name, and explain basic science concepts with confidence
- High re-watch value and parent-child bonding
- Strong foundation for later grades
- YouTube growth through educational + entertainment value (CoComelon-style appeal + Tamil cultural authenticity)

**Common Production DNA (All 10 Songs):**
- **Characters (consistent across series):** Meenu (curious 6yo girl scientist — host), Kutty (energetic 6yo boy friend — comic relief), Teacher Kavitha Miss (warm 32yo guide)
- **Animation Style:** 3D Pixar cinematic lighting, subsurface scattering, ultra-detailed, child-friendly, 8K
- **Music:** Suno AI (15 style variations provided per song), 106–114 BPM, C major, ukulele + marimba + children’s chorus core
- **Duration:** Exactly 3:30 (21 scenes × 10 seconds)
- **Screen Text Rule:** ALL on-screen words in **ENGLISH ONLY**
- **Song Language:** Natural bilingual — Tamil descriptive lines + English science keywords woven in
- **Viral Hook + Empowerment Ending:** Every song ends with “YOU are a SCIENTIST now!” + gold badge moment
