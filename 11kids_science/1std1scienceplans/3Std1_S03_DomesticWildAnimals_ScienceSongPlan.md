## 🧠 TOPIC OVERVIEW

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| **Science Concept** | Domestic Animals vs Wild Animals — where they live and how they depend on humans |
| **Standard** | Grade 1 / Standard 1 |
| **Tamil Term** | வீட்டு விலங்குகள் (Domestic) / காட்டு விலங்குகள் (Wild) |
| **English Term** | Domestic Animals / Wild Animals |
| **Why Third?** | After learning what is Living vs Non-Living and how plants are built, children now classify the animals they see every day — some live close to us (domestic), some live freely in nature (wild). This builds observation and classification skills |
| **Viral Hook** | "Does it LIVE WITH US at home or on the farm? → DOMESTIC! Does it LIVE FREE in the forest or jungle? → WILD!" |
| **Screen Text Rule** | ALL words displayed on screen are in ENGLISH only |
| **Song Language** | Bilingual — Tamil + English words woven together naturally |

## 🎓 SCIENCE CONCEPTS TAUGHT IN THIS SONG

| Concept | Tamil | English (On Screen) | Simple Explanation for Kids |
|---|---|---|---|
| Domestic Animals | வீட்டு விலங்குகள் | DOMESTIC ANIMALS | Animals that live with humans. We give them food, water, and a safe home. They often help us too! |
| Wild Animals | காட்டு விலங்குகள் | WILD ANIMALS | Animals that live in forests and jungles. They find their own food and make their own homes. They are free! |
| Pet | செல்லப் பிராணி | PET | A special animal friend who lives inside our house (Dog, Cat, Rabbit) |
| Farm Animal | பண்ணை விலங்கு | FARM ANIMAL | Animals that live on a farm and help us by giving milk, eggs, or wool (Cow, Goat, Hen) |
| Forest / Jungle | காடு | FOREST | The big home full of trees where wild animals live freely and safely |
| Live with us | நம்முடன் வாழும் | LIVE WITH US | Domestic animals stay close to people |
| Live in the wild | காட்டில் வாழும் | LIVE IN THE WILD | Wild animals live independently in nature |
