## 🎓 SCIENCE CONCEPTS TAUGHT IN THIS SONG

| Concept | Tamil | English (On Screen) | Simple Function (for kids) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Root | வேர் | ROOT | Drinks water & nutrients from soil; holds plant tight |
| Stem | தண்டு | STEM | Holds the plant up straight; carries water and food up & down |
| Leaf | இலை | LEAF | Catches sunlight and makes food for the plant |
| Flower | பூ | FLOWER | Makes seeds; pretty colours attract bees and butterflies |
| Fruit | பழம் | FRUIT | Protects the seeds inside; some fruits are yummy to eat |
| Seed | விதை | SEED | Grows into a brand new plant (baby plant) |
